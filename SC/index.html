      How to use Student Companion

      Dashboard

      Your home screen. Shows pending tasks, course count, and current GPA at a glance. The reminder updates based on how many tasks you still have open.

      Tasks

      Add any assignment or to-do with a name, due date, and priority level. Mark items done with the Done button — they get crossed out but stay visible. The pending count feeds into your dashboard.

      Timetable

      Build your weekly schedule by picking a day, entering a course name, setting a start time, choosing a duration (30 min up to 3 hours), and optionally adding a venue. Classes appear in a grid with days across the top and times on the left. Turn on Saturday and Sunday with the toggles at the top. Give your timetable a name, then hit Export PDF for a clean printable copy.

      Grade Tracker

      Enter each course code, your score out of 100, and the credit hours. The app converts your score to a letter grade and grade points using the UG grading scale, then calculates your weighted GPA automatically.

      UG Grading Scale A (80–100, 4.0)  ·  B+ (75–79, 3.5)  ·  B (70–74, 3.0)  ·  C+ (65–69, 2.5)  ·  C (60–64, 2.0)  ·  D+ (55–59, 1.5)  ·  D (50–54, 1.0)  ·  F (0–49, 0.0)
      Notes

      Save quick reminders, ideas, or study summaries as titled cards. Remove any note with the × button.

      Slides

      Upload lecture slides or any course material by clicking the upload area or dragging files onto it. You can upload multiple files at once. Each file prompts you to tag it with a course name for easy filtering. Files are stored in your browser and can be opened or downloaded at any time.

      Data & Privacy

      Everything is saved in your browser's local storage on your own device — nothing is sent anywhere. Clearing your browser's site data will erase it, so use the timetable PDF export as a backup for your schedule.
